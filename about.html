About Us

NITOR Carpentry is a full-service construction company specializing in residential building and remodeling. Our company brings over 25 years of building experience and we ensure successful projects by working closely with our clients and a carefully chosen group of subcontractors and suppliers. We welcome and encourage client participation at every step along the way.

Our Team

At NITOR, you'll find a seasoned staff of knowledgeable, dedicated and friendly construction industry professionals who are focused on providing reliable, professional service and outstanding solutions that you can count on.

By encouraging an honest, open and collaborative work environment, we have developed a team that provides great value, consistent direction and responsiveness to the challenging demands of the residential construction industry. Our team approach has proven itself time and time again with the successful completion of a vast number of completed projects and satisfied customers.

Sub Contractors

Although we do all of the construction work, we do hire sub contractors for the electrical, plumbing, heating, cooling, and some masonry portions of the project. We have been working with the same companies for years and are confident that their work will be completed when needed and you will be pleased with the results. All of the sub contractors that we use are licensed and insured.
