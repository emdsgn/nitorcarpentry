Additions & Renovations

When it comes to renovations and additions, we are your subject matter experts. Our experienced and qualified team will ensure proper and precise planning to eliminate costly surprises, and do the job correctly, on-time and on-budget.

Nitor is a full-service building company that specializes in providing turn-key solutions, and this is more important in additions and renovations than anywhere else. Re-modelling or integrating new and old require many more unique building solutions and finishing touches than new construction does.

Nitor has a long history in providing not only excellent craftsmanship, but professional and thorough solutions and service delivery. Nitor employs all the expertise required to complete all aspects of your project from design, construction, and all finishing details. This allows projects to be completed more quickly and cost effectively than our competition, while providing the highest product quality and workmanship.

We can build-to-print, or we can start from scratch and work with you on design, materials selection, schedule and budget projections, to ensure that your project meets all of your expectations
