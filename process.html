OVERALL PLAN

Our education process is one we are most proud of at NITOR. Since every project is unique, we will walk you through how the project can be done on time, and on budget. We will always start with a complementary quote for your project, and we will take the time to truly understanding your building needs. Over the years working in Ottawa, we have learned that our planning process is very unique, detailed and disciplined. We will work directly with you to create a project plan, which includes timelines, budget, and creative opportunities to be sure we help you build the custom home, make the right addition and / or renovate the way you want.

Design

From scratch, we will embrace your ideas, and help them flourish with our initial drawings, and we will continue to develop them in to the most effective design based on your tastes and your needs.
