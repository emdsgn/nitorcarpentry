Planning

It starts with getting a free quote. Each project is unique, so we always start by learning. Our experts will discuss your needs with you to make sure we understand what you’re looking for, your style, budget, and your home.

Our goal is to create a room you will love to use, and because we’re committed to high quality our attention to detail starts here. Our team at Nitor Carpentry will work with you to create a personal project plan, including a timeline and budget, which gives you the renovation you want.

Design

We pride ourselves on giving you the look and feel that you want. Whether it’s a kitchen renovation, bathroom renovation, basement finish or new home addition, our professional designers will create a thoughtful, and professionally crafted proposal just for you. We can create a detailed plan for our construction team once you’re fully satisfied. This will be used to successfully bring your project to completion on time, and on budget.

Once the build process begins, we will coordinate with surveyors, engineers, and city officials to obtain the necessary permits to ensure your project is completed properly up to the Ontario Building Code standards. From the initial design concepts to final construction drawings, our team of expert designers are here to help you with your renovation project.

Build

At Ottawa General Contractors, we build to last and we stand by our quality work. Each of our renovations comes with an industry-leading 5-year warranty – but we don’t think you’ll need it. From sourcing the right materials for new walls to fitting the kitchen sink, we have the right person for the job. As the build progresses, you’ll see different professionals working on your property, from engineers to painters, and decorators – each skilled in their chosen field.

Part of our full-service approach means that you won’t have to worry about finding a qualified electrician or a skilled bathroom fitter – we’ve carefully vetted all the people we work with, giving you confidence that you’re working with certified experts. With a whole team focusing on your renovation, you get top of the line communication. Throughout the project, we’ll keep you up-to-date on how your renovation is progressing, and you’ll be able to come to us with any questions or queries.
